About Us

Rubies Delight Cakes and Confectionaries is a bakery business located in Ibadan, Nigeria. Founded by Chairman and CEO Temitiope Abiodun in the year 2013, the bakery has expanded over the past 12 years, delivering customer satisfaction as well as providing delicious cakes and treats no matter the ocassion.
